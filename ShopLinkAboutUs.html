ABOUT US

We are a team of Engineering students with a Zeal to solve real life problems.

We today see that a lot of retailers selling potentially perishable products face difficulty selling their complete stock before they expire.

To fastrack this process of clearing the stock and give atleast some profit to the seller, we created this portal which lists down close to expiry products of our Affiliate Retailers at a lower price to save the retailers from loss and at the same time provide very low rates for products.
